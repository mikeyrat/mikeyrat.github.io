About Your United States Navy Blue Angels

The Blue Angels were formed in 1946 to help raise awareness and public interest in naval aviation. Currently one of the Navy's most effective and visible recruitment tools, the Blue Angels perform approximately 32 air shows a year around the United States. Flying the new and more powerful F/A-18 Super Hornet, the team is known for its precision formation aerobatics and other maneuvers that demonstrate not only the abilities and discipline of naval aviators, but also the performance and flexible capabilities of their aircraft.
